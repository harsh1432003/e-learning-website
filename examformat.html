Exam Protocol

Please read and understand ICTRD exam Protocols carefully before appearing for exam

  • This exam has 30 questions. 2 marks for each answer, thus this is a total of 60 marks exam
  • Passing criteria is 50%
  • *Do not appear exam between 10:00PM to 1:00AM
  • The exam is "Online from Anywhere," allowing you to take it from any location within the allotted period.
  • Candidate have to make sure that he has good and flawless internet connections at-least for one hour before initiating exam.
  • System will not give extra time in case on any interruption once you start your exam.

    For Example,
    If you start your exam on 5:00 PM, then system will automatically note end time as 6:PM and exam solutions will be submitted automatically once it reached end time. That means, If you mistakenly logged out the dashboard, you can re-login it but your transition time (when you are offline on dashboard) will also been added to your exam and it will be submitted once it reached one hour. candidate can submit his solution before time also.
  • The exam is timezone based. It only supports Indian time.
  • System will automatically generate Certificate, mark-sheet and budge after successful completion of exam in case of Pass Grade. Only Mark-Sheet will be generated in case of failed candidates.
  • Candidate can find his solution after exam. he can recheck or validate his solutions after exam.
  • ICTRD will not been held responsible for any gains and losses happened due to use of this system or by not following protocols.
  • candidates are allowed to speak with any ICTRD assigned expert on via phone call during exam for any technical support regarding system.
  • Candidate can apply for extra attempts by Paying Rs. 650 each. from their dashboard.
  • Only individuals can apply for this certification course. No institutional registration is allowed. If such registration is found, it will be kept on hold and will not be eligible for a refund.
